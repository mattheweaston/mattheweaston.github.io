I am an Associate at Cornerstone Research in New York City.

I received a PhD from Columbia University in 2025, where I wrote my dissertation in the areas of urban and spatial economics. Prior, I worked at the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago from 2016 to 2019 and received a BS in Mathematics from Penn State in 2016.
